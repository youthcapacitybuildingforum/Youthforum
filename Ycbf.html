About YCBTF Uganda

Who We Are

The Youth Capacity Trainers Forum – Uganda (YCBTF Uganda) is a non-profit, community-based initiative founded in 2018 with a singular vision: to empower the young people of Uganda. We believe that an informed, skilled, and healthy youth population is the cornerstone of a thriving nation.

Our journey began with a small team of dedicated volunteers who recognized the critical need for comprehensive youth development beyond traditional schooling. From humble beginnings in local community halls, we've grown into a recognized force for positive change, reaching thousands of youth across various districts.

Our Vision

To nurture an informed, skilled, and healthy youth population in Uganda, capable of driving sustainable development and positive societal change.

Our Mission

To equip young people in schools and communities with essential tools for a purposeful life through holistic knowledge, capacity building, health awareness, and guidance programs.

Our Core Values

  • Empathy
  • Integrity
  • Community
  • Excellence
  • Youth-Led Change

Covers transport costs for youth from remote areas to attend a full day of training.

UGX 150,000 for Refreshments

Ensures youth receive nutritious snacks and drinks during long training sessions.
