Your Gift, Their Future: Donate Today

Every shilling you give ignites hope and creates lasting change for Ugandan youth.

See Your Impact

Why Your Support Matters Now More Than Ever

In Uganda, countless young people face immense challenges – from limited access to quality education and vital health information, to lack of opportunities for skill development and meaningful employment. These barriers hinder their potential and impact the future of our nation.

At YCBF Uganda, we stand in this gap. We believe that with the right tools, knowledge, and support, every young person can overcome adversity and build a brighter future. Your donation is not just a contribution; it's an investment in a generation poised to lead, innovate, and transform their communities.

Your compassion can break cycles of poverty, empower voices, and unlock the incredible potential within every young Ugandan. **Join us in this urgent mission.**

See the Direct Impact of Your Generosity

Even the smallest contribution creates a ripple effect. Here’s what your donation can achieve:

UGX 2,000

School Supplies for a Week

Provides essential pens, notebooks, and learning materials, ensuring a child can actively participate in school.

UGX 5,000

Health Awareness Session

Funds a crucial health awareness workshop for 10 youth, covering vital topics like hygiene and SRHR.

UGX 10,000

Digital Literacy Training

Supports one young person through a foundational digital literacy module, opening doors to the digital world.

UGX 25,000

Monthly Mentorship Support

Provides one month of dedicated mentorship for a youth, guiding them through personal and professional development.

UGX 50,000

Entrepreneurship Workshop Kit

Equips a small group of youth with materials for a hands-on entrepreneurship workshop, fostering innovation.

UGX 100,000

Mini-Library Establishment

Contributes significantly to establishing or stocking a mini-library in a rural school, promoting literacy.

Every contribution, no matter the size, fuels our mission and transforms lives. Thank you for your kindness.
